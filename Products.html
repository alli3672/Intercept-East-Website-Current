Privacy Policy

Intercept East is committed to protecting your privacy. We handle personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, company name, and enquiry details.

We may also collect limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited to improve website performance.

How We Use Information
  • Respond to enquiries and requests
  • Provide information about our products and services
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Meet legal and regulatory obligations
Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared with trusted service providers or disclosed where required by law.

Data Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Information is retained only as long as necessary.

Cookies

This website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Access & Corrections

You may request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.
