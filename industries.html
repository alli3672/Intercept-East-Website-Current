Industries We Serve

Intercept East Pty Ltd provides clean, residue-free preservation for metals, electronics, and sensitive equipment. Our solutions prevent corrosion and contamination during storage, transport, and long-term lay-up — helping you reduce rework, protect uptime, and operate more sustainably.

Where we add the most value

  • Mining shutdown kits, cabinets, and critical spares
  • Defence avionics, optics, and deployable assets
  • Electronics/PCBs with ESD & ionic contamination risks
  • Museum artefacts and conservation storage

What you can expect

  • Multi-year corrosion control in coastal climates
  • Oil-free, inspection-ready packaging
  • Lower preservation cost and reduced waste
  • Clean options suited to sensitive materials
