How We Helped Save Over $1M

Enhancing Equipment Preservation & Storage Efficiency

How one heavy industry operation unlocked $1M+ in annual savings through strategic warehousing improvements.

The Challenge

  • Deteriorating packaging causing corrosion and contamination
  • Poor inventory control with excess stock and lost components
  • Exposure of hydraulic hoses and metal parts accelerating wear
  • Disorganised spaces increasing retrieval times

The Solution

  • Upgraded packaging with UV-stabilised wrap and crate protection
  • Smarter inventory practices using FIFO and surplus review
  • Indoor storage and caps for hydraulic and metal components
  • Layout optimisation with zones, signage, and container conversion

Estimated Financial Impact

Category Estimated Annual Savings
Reduced waste & damaged equipment$200,000/year
Faster retrieval times & labour efficiency$75,000–$100,000/year
Reduced asset replacement & disposal costs$200,000–$300,000/year
Extended lifespan of critical components$400,000/year
Total Estimated Annual Savings $875,000–$1,000,000+

Conclusion

This case study shows how practical changes in packaging, inventory, and layout transformed operations — driving nearly $1M in annual savings and extending asset lifecycles.
